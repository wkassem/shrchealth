About SHRC Health

Our Mission

We are dedicated to providing comprehensive, patient-centered rehabilitation and wellness services. Our experienced team specializes in helping patients recover, improve mobility, and achieve optimal health outcomes through evidence-based treatment methods.

With state-of-the-art facilities and compassionate care, we serve patients of all ages, from acute injuries to chronic conditions.

Our Services

Physiotherapy

Evidence-based physical therapy to restore mobility, reduce pain, and improve function through targeted exercises and manual therapy techniques.

Osteopathy

Holistic osteopathic treatment addressing musculoskeletal issues to improve body mechanics and overall wellness.

Acupuncture

Traditional acupuncture therapy to relieve pain, reduce inflammation, and promote natural healing and relaxation.

Group Classes

Specialized group sessions including rehabilitation exercises, wellness programs, and preventative health workshops.

Injury Assessment

Comprehensive evaluation and diagnosis of injuries with detailed treatment recommendations and recovery planning.

Rehabilitation Programs

Customized rehabilitation programs for post-surgical recovery, sports injuries, and chronic conditions.
